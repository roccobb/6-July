# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-24

Finnish Word of the Day by Valkotassu does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app works offline. It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, user accounts, push notifications, or network requests.

Favorites are stored locally on your device using Android app storage. They are not sent to Valkotassu or any third party.

If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated before the new behavior is released.

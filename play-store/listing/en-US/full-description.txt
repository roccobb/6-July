Finnish Word of the Day helps you discover one Finnish word each day with a clear English definition.

The app works offline and keeps things simple: open it, read today's word, save favorites, and share words with friends.

Features:
- One Finnish word selected each day
- English definitions
- Optional pronunciation and example sentences when available
- Favorites saved locally on your device
- Share the daily word using Android's native share menu
- No account required
- No ads, analytics, or tracking

Dictionary data is derived from Wiktionary contributors via Kaikki.org/Wiktextract. This app is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by the Wikimedia Foundation, Wiktionary, Kaikki.org, or Wiktextract.
